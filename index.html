Welcome to Lop 6/7 TK.

Lop 6/7 TK is a big service with product: Lop 6/7 Tim Kiem (Search Engine), 6/7 Lop Toi (Offical website of Lop 6/7 TK), Qua Nhung Con So (A Flappy Bird + 2048 clone game) and others... You can help us by translate, report a bug,... We're happy when you help us.

Lop 6/7 Tim Kiem (Lop 6/7 Search)

Release date: 12/6/2013 Language: Vietnamese (but support English and others language when search) Lop 6/7 Tim Kiem is a vietnamese search engine use for family and students, It's provide you a safe environment on internet, no more worry about harmful content to your child, your students. We're currently support Web, Images, Videos, News and Books search. Also support on small phone screen and an Windows App is coming soon. http://www.lop67.tk

Lop 6/7 Hoi Dap (Lop 6/7 Ask and Answer)

Release date: 21/3/2015 (I guess) Language: Vietnamese(but support English and others language when se Lop 6/7 Hoi Dap help you ask or answer a question online and free. Got question just come here and search! Bingo! You got the answer! http://www.lop67.tk/hoidap

Qua Nhung Con So (English mean Fly through the numbers)

Language: Vietnamese, English Support: PC, Android and IOS Qua Nhung Con So (English mean "Fly through the numbers"), a game for study math or practice mouse skill. It's a fun game, you should try it at: http://www.lop67.tk/quanhungconso
